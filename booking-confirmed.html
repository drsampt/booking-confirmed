How this works

Top Performance PT is not a traditional clinic. There is no waiting room, no shared treatment table, no clock running out after 30 minutes. Every session is one-on-one and it happens in your gym, your home, or wherever you actually train.

The clinical philosophy is simple: rehab should look like training. You are not getting pulled off the bar. Your programming is not getting replaced with band walks and clamshells. The goal is to find what is actually driving your pain or limitation under load, address it directly, and get you back to lifting at full capacity as fast as possible and then past it.

Your roadmap
01
Assessment

Find the root cause. Not just where it hurts, but why it is happening under the specific demands of your training. Movement quality, load tolerance, and sport-specific positions are all on the table.

02
Symptom Reduction

Address what is limiting you now without taking you out of training. Modifications are built around your program, not the other way around. You keep lifting while the underlying issue gets resolved.

03
Foundation Building

Once pain is managed, close the gaps. Strength asymmetries, movement deficiencies, and positions that break down under load. This is where long-term durability gets built.

04
Performance Optimization

This is where most PT stops. We do not. Once back at baseline, the work shifts toward making you a more resilient and stronger athlete than you were before the injury.

Before your session
How to prepare
  • Wear what you would train in. If a specific lift has been painful, be ready to move through it.
  • Know when the issue started and what makes it better or worse under load.
  • Have a sense of what your training has looked like around the injury. Volume, frequency, and any recent changes to programming or intensity.
  • Be specific. The more detail you bring to the session, the faster we move past assessment and into actual work.
  • Any questions before the session, reply to the confirmation email or reach out directly through the Practice Better app.

Looking forward to getting to work. If anything comes up before the session, do not hesitate to reach out.
